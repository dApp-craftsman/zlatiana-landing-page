The wisdom behind it all

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is a 5,000-year-old system of natural healing from India. Its name comes from the Sanskrit words "ayur" (life) and "veda" (knowledge or science) — quite literally, the science of life. Rather than treating symptoms, Ayurveda looks at you as a whole: body, mind and spirit, and helps you live in rhythm with nature.

☯

Your Unique Constitution

Ayurveda teaches that every person has a unique mind-body type (dosha) — Vata, Pitta or Kapha — and that true wellness comes from understanding and balancing your own nature, not following a one-size-fits-all plan.

🌿

Food as Medicine

What, how and when you eat matters just as much as what you eat. Ayurveda offers simple, seasonal and nourishing food principles that support digestion, energy and emotional balance.
